Popular Cafes

The most popular cafe

In 2018, the first permanent Pokémon Café opened in Japan with great fanfare. It's a very stylish place, moderately decorated, and at the same time very bright. It pleases guests and fans with incredibly cute dishes, including carbonara in the shape of Pikachu's butt, and a Bulbasaur burger, and a Gengar smoothie in a backlit glass with a sly grin.

The café also sells souvenirs for friends and family, such as Pokémon pasta and Pikachu cookies.
